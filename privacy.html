Privacy Policy for Diary application

Last Updated: 08-Apr-2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Diary application. We value your privacy and the sanctity of your personal thoughts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data (Diary Entries):

Local Storage: Diary application is designed as a local-first application. Your diary entries, photos are stored directly on your device's internal storage and are not uploaded to our servers.

Permissions:

    The application may request access to your device's storage to save your entries and backup files.
    The application may request access to pick up photos on the phone because you may wanted to store some pictures with you diary entry.
    The application may request access to location service the phone because it requires to have longitude and latitude of you current location for getting weather and address information.
    The application may request access to have internet access on the phone because we need to query external web sites to have weather and address information.

Automatically Collected Data:

We do not collect personal identifiers or track your location.

3. Data Storage and Security

Because your data is stored locally on your Android device:

Security: The security of your data depends on your device's security settings (biometrics, PIN, or password).

Backups: If you use the "Save to MD file" or "Export" function to backup your data for reading or migration to another phone, the resulting file is stored on your device's Download folder. You are responsible for deleting them from Download folder after usage.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools that collect information used to identify you:

Google Play Services: Used for app distribution and updates.

Weather information API: We call API in https://api.open-meteo.com/v1/ together weather information in your current location.

Address information API: We use API in android.location.Geocoder package to get address information.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can edit or delete your diary entries at any time within the app. Deleting the app will also delete all locally stored data unless a manual backup has been created.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
